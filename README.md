# ⚡ aGi Skills: High-Performance AI System Prompts

> "If a machine can do it faster, cheaper & scalable, we automate it. If an AI talks too much, we Caveman it."

---

## 🛠️ The Philosophy
At **aGi**, we don't do fluff. We build systems that perform. Most AI today is tuned for politeness and verbosity—wasting tokens and your time. **aGi Skills** is a library of curated system prompts designed to turn your LLMs into razor-sharp technical tools that speak the language of absolute efficiency.

### 🏗️ Build vs. Buy: The Prompt Engineering Reality
- **The "Buy" Trap (SaaS Wrappers)**: Most generic AI "productivity" tools are just expensive blankets over public APIs. They force a one-size-fits-all persona that hallucinations often and explains the obvious.
- **The "Build" Edge (Custom Prompts)**: By engineering your own system instructions, you reduce latency, cut token costs by up to 90%, and ensure the AI respects your specific architectural constraints. Pro developers don't need a "chat buddy"—they need an execution engine.
